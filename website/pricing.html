Common questions

How does it work?

How do you measure "verified savings"?
+
We baseline your cost per agent over a 14-day window, then measure reduction after applying our recommendations. Savings are the difference between your baseline trajectory and actual spend, adjusted for volume changes. You can see the exact calculation in your dashboard at any time.
What if my costs go down for unrelated reasons?
+
We only attribute savings to Traeco recommendations that were applied. If your costs drop because you shipped fewer requests, we credit that to volume change, not Traeco. Our attribution model is conservative by design — we’d rather under-bill than overcharge.
What does "zero savings, zero charge" mean exactly?
+
If Traeco’s recommendations don’t measurably reduce your AI costs in a given month, you pay $0 for that month. There are no platform fees, seat fees, or minimum charges. We eat the cost of running the platform until we can prove value.
Can I see my projected savings before committing?
+
Yes. During your 30-minute onboarding call, we run a read-only analysis of your existing traces (or a representative sample) and give you a projected monthly savings estimate before you wire anything up. No obligation.
Which providers does Traeco support?
+
OpenAI, Anthropic, Google (Gemini), Cohere, and Mistral are fully supported. We have experimental support for Azure OpenAI and AWS Bedrock. If you’re using a provider not listed, reach out — we add providers within two weeks when there’s demand.
How long does integration take?
+
Most teams are ingesting traces within 30 minutes. Add 3 lines of code, point to your API key, and the first insights appear within 24 hours. See the integration guide for the exact steps.
